How to play

Collect gold and level up by fighting monsters and finding treasure in this simple dungeon crawler!

As you explore the dungeon, you will encounter monsters that you may choose to fight. You may obtain valuable treasure and healing potions by opening treasure chests, but be wary of deadly traps that may be hidden within. The game ends when you leave the dungeon or succumb to the dangers within.For detailed instructions, see the Design page.
